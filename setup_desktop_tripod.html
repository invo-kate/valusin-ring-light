Set up the desktop tripod

Four simple steps to set up the desktop tripod for use with the Valusin™ 10" ring light.

  1. Attach the ball head.
    Align the ball head on the screw at the top of the desktop tripod, and twist until it becomes tight.

  2. Attach the ring light to the tripod.
    Align the screw on top of the tripod to the hole in the bottom of the ring light and twist until secure.

  3. Adjust the ring light using the ball head.
    Twist the screw on the side of the ball head to rotate and tilt the ring light. Tighten the screw once the ring light is in the desired position.

  4. Desktop tripod setup is complete.
