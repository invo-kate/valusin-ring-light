DANGER
Danger of electric shock
Improper use of this product can electric shock.
Do not use this product near water, on damp surfaces, or anywhere water is present.
Do not touch the power cord with wet hands.
Do not use this product if it is broken, damaged or has exposed wires.
Never attempt to insert metal objects not associated with setup into the ring light.
Only use this product indoors.
