Button Functions

Button functions on the 10" Valusin™ ring light.

Power

Click to turn the ring light on and off.

Increase Brightness

Click to turn up the brightness level from 1-8.

Decrease Brightness

Click to turn down the brightness level from 8-1.

Enable Standard Mode

Click to turn on standard light mode. While in standard light mode, click to rotate through white, natural, and warm light.

Enable RGB Mode

Click to turn RGB light mode on and off. While in RGB light mode, click to rotate through the fourteen distinct light colors. Click and hold for 3-5 seconds to enter gradient mode, where the ring light slowly cycles through the colors.
